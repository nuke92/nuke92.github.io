Important information

Every museum visitor must be able to show a valid admission ticket. This also applies to your baby, your companion or if you have free entrance.

Always bring your (valid) discount pass to the museum. Without a scan of this pass, your discounted ticket will not be valid. If you are under 18 years old, you may be asked to provide identification.
